All About Emmett Martin"

I chose Babson mainly because of its diverse community, the rigorous academics, diversified business curriculum and the opportunity to compete on a competitive division 3 lacrosse team. I thought that going to a college that is not only national, but also international would expose me to different ways of thinking and better me. Babson was a highly ranked school, but more than the prestige the diversity of coursework was attractive to me as I was a high schooler interested in business but unsure what exactly I wanted to pursue. Lastly, the opportunity to compete on a nationally ranked lacrosse team was attractive to me as I loved to play and am a competitive person. Those are the key factors that drove me to attending Babson.

My favorite sport is, and has been for many years, the game of lacrosse. I have played since I was in kindergarten, so about 17 years. The sport was not my first love as I grew up loving the game of hockey and was obsessed with the Boston Bruins, but by the time I reached middle school I knew I wanted to take it more seriously. I played on nationally competitive travel and high school teams which was a blast in high school. I love to play the game in large part due to the fact that it is great exercise, it requires cardio, hand eye coordination, full body strength and mobility, and IQ. As I have gotten older I have come to appreciate the community and friendships I have built through the game. I am close to hundreds of people that I have played, with or against which I find quite special.
